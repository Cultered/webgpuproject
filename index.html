WebGPU Required

Your browser does not have WebGPU enabled. Please follow these steps:

  • Update your browser to the latest version.
  • For Chrome/Edge: Go to chrome://flags/#enable-unsafe-webgpu, enable the flag, and restart.
  • For Firefox Nightly: Go to about:config, set dom.webgpu.enabled to true, and restart.
  • For Safari: Enable WebGPU in the Develop menu or Feature Flags.
